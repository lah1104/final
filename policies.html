Organizational Policies

  • Food Safety Standards: We will strictly adhere to food safety regulations to prevent contamination and spoilage.
  • Inspection: All donated food will be inspected for quality, freshness, and safety.
  • Storage: Food will be stored in clean, cool, and dry conditions to prevent spoilage.
  • Expiration Dates: Food items past their expiration date will be discarded.
  • Proper Handling: Food handlers will follow strict hygiene protocols to prevent contamination.
  • Local Partnerships: We will collaborate with local organizations to identify areas of need and coordinate distribution efforts.
  • Background Checks: Volunteers may be required to undergo background checks.
  • Training: Volunteers will receive training on food safety, hygiene, and proper handling procedures.
  • Supervision: Volunteers will be supervised by experienced staff members.
  • Community Input: We will actively seek input from the community to ensure that our delivery services meet their specific needs.
  • Flexible Scheduling: We will be flexible in our delivery schedules to accommodate the needs of our clients.
  • Contingency Plans: We will develop contingency plans to address potential disruptions to our delivery services, such as inclement weather or supply chain issues.
